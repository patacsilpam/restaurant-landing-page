About us

Mang Mac’s Marinero Food Shop is known for its best pizza shop in Urdaneta City, Pangasinan. Mang Mac's Foodshop is a restaurant located at McArthur Highway in front of BHF Appliance Center. It also has other franchise located at Zone 5, Brgy. Sta Lucia Bypass Road, Urdaneta City, Pangasinan.

Mang Macs Pizza House is a family business of the Castro family. Mr. Mark O. Castro, a seaman and the owner of Mang Mac’s Foodshop. The Castro Family decided to change the Mang Mac’s Pizza House to Mang Mac’s Food Shop, added some dish and another flavor of pizzas in the menu.

When you visit the restaurant, our team gladly serves you from our best menus with several flavors of pizza, pasta, burger and meals. We believe in the maxim, serving you with our dishes is one of the best ways to "bring happiness in your table."
